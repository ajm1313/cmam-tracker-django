🚨 MAM Exclusion: Infant Under 6 Months

Infants less than 6 months are NOT admitted for MAM management.

  • If complications or poor suckling → Refer to Hospital/IPC
  • If no complications, breastfeeding possible → Manage via SAM OPC infant-at-risk pathway

Please do not proceed with MAM registration for this infant.
